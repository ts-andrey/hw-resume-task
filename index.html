About myself

After I graduate high school I didn't know where to go and I didn't found my passion yet. So among all available paths I chose to become an engineer. It was very interesting experience for me throughout which I got technical way of thinking and learned how to solve problems. In 2016 I got my master in energetics and in the process I get a little taste of programming and I liked it, but wasn't able to follow my passion due to my family issues.

In 2021 I finally got my chance to follow my dream and I chose for myself front-end specialty. I found good course that satisfied my expectations and gave me a lot of practice. It wasn't an easy course, but in the same time it brought me more enjoyment after each task completion. During the courses I really enjoyed spending my time behind coding and some of my projects have been recognized as a best ones among the people I have studied with.

My goal is endless improvement in order to become the best developer I can to get more possibilities in providing best services and help to found the most effective solutions for any kind of problems because I like to learn and to help.
